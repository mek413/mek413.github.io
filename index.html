Glory Sound Prep My New Favorite Album

Jon Bellion's lastest album Glory Sound Prep released on November 9, 2018. This album featured songs from a variety of different styles. Coming off his last album, The Human Condition, many fans were anticipating his next album. However, Jon did not disappoint us fans with the release of Glory Sound Prep. Before we jump into the tracklist, lets me give a quick bio on Jon Bellion.


Bio

Jonathan Bellion is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. He was born and raised in Lake Grove, New York on Long Island. He is known for his single "All Time Low". Bellion has released four mixtapes and two studio albums. His first, The Human Condition, was released on June 10, 2016, and it debuted at number five on the US Billboard 200. He released his second studio album, Glory Sound Prep, on November 9, 2018. He is currently signed to Visionary Music Group and Capitol Records. Bellion also provided vocals for the 2015 EDM song "Beautiful Now" by Zedd and opened the concerts from the third leg of Twenty One Pilots' Emotional Roadshow World Tour.
